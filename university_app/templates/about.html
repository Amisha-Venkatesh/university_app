About Us

Welcome to our platform! We are dedicated to providing you with the most accurate and up-to-date information about university rankings worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer students, educators, and institutions a comprehensive tool for evaluating and comparing universities globally. Through detailed and transparent ranking systems, we aim to help users make informed decisions about higher education options.

Why Choose Us?

We stand out by providing a user-friendly interface combined with reliable data sources. Whether you're a student looking for the best university or an academic institution trying to boost its rankings, we offer the resources you need to succeed.

How We Calculate Rankings

Our ranking methodology considers multiple critical factors to ensure a fair and accurate assessment of each university. These factors include:

  • Alumni Impact: The achievements and contributions of alumni in their respective fields.
  • Awards: Prestigious awards received by faculty and alumni.
  • Research Excellence (HiCi): The number of highly cited researchers affiliated with the university.
  • Research Output (N&S): Publications in top-tier journals and conferences.
  • Publications (PUB): The total volume of research articles published.
  • Per Capita Performance (PCP): Performance metrics adjusted per faculty member.
